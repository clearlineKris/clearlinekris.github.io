# Letter of the Law Stack (LOTL) — Colorado

> **Status:** `[TEMPLATE]` — Outline-level scaffolding only  
> **IR Package:** Colorado Intelligence Rollout ([Issue #18](https://github.com/clearlineKris/clearlinekris.github.io/issues/18))

## Purpose

Full statutory and regulatory text reference stack for Colorado's cannabis regulations. The comprehensive, organized collection of every applicable law, rule, and regulation — the literal "letter of the law."

---

## Outline

### 1. Stack Organization

The LOTL is organized in a hierarchy:
1. **Constitutional provisions** (highest authority)
2. **State statutes** (C.R.S.)
3. **Administrative rules** (CCR)
4. **Agency guidance** (bulletins, advisories, interpretive memos)
5. **Local ordinances** (municipal/county-specific)

### 2. Constitutional Layer

- Colorado Constitution, Article XVIII, Section 14 — Medical marijuana
- Colorado Constitution, Article XVIII, Section 16 — Amendment 64
- Relevant federal constitutional considerations (Commerce Clause, Supremacy Clause)

### 3. Statutory Layer

- C.R.S. § 44-10-101 through 44-10-1003 — Full Colorado Marijuana Code
  - Part 1: General provisions and definitions
  - Part 2: Licensing
  - Part 3: Operational requirements
  - Part 4: Enforcement and penalties
  - Part 5: Local government authority
  - Part 6: Unlicensed activity
- C.R.S. § 35-61-101 et seq. — Industrial Hemp
- C.R.S. § 39-28.8-101 et seq. — Marijuana tax
- Related criminal provisions (C.R.S. Title 18)

### 4. Administrative Rule Layer

- 1 CCR 212-3 — MED Rules
  - Series R: Retail marijuana
  - Series M: Medical marijuana
  - Series C: Common provisions
  - Series T: Testing
  - Series E: Enforcement
- CDPHE rules on product safety
- CDA rules on industrial hemp

### 5. Agency Guidance Layer

- MED industry bulletins (indexed by topic and date)
- DOR tax guidance documents
- CDPHE product safety advisories
- Attorney General opinions on cannabis

### 6. Local Ordinance Layer

- City and County of Denver
- Colorado Springs
- Boulder
- Aurora
- Additional municipalities (indexed)

### 7. Federal Reference Layer

- 21 U.S.C. § 812 — Controlled Substances Act schedules
- 7 U.S.C. § 1639o — 2018 Farm Bill hemp definition
- Relevant DOJ memoranda and policy statements
- FDA enforcement discretion letters
